Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Switcherooinator9000 is a playlist converter app that moves playlists between Spotify and Apple Music. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data We Collect

We collect nothing. The app does not transmit any data to the developer or any third-party server operated by us.

Third-Party Services

The app connects directly to Spotify and Apple Music on your behalf using your own accounts. When you authorize the app:

  • A Spotify OAuth access token and refresh token are stored locally on your device.
  • Apple Music authorization is managed by the operating system through MusicKit.

All playlist data (track names, artists, etc.) is fetched directly from Spotify or Apple Music and used only to perform the conversion. It is never stored beyond the active session or sent anywhere other than the destination service.

Your use of Spotify is subject to Spotify's Privacy Policy. Your use of Apple Music is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

The app stores the following on your device only:

  • Your Spotify refresh token (in local app storage), so you stay logged in between sessions.
  • A local history of your past conversions (playlist names and match results).

You can clear this data at any time by disconnecting your accounts in the app's Settings or by deleting the app.

Updates

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
